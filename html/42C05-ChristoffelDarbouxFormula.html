Christoffel-Darboux formula


Let {ϕi}i=0n be orthonormal polynomials (the degree of ϕk is k) and let kn be the coefficient of xn in ϕn. Then

∑k=0nϕk⁢(x)⁢ϕk⁢(y)=knkn+1⁢(ϕn⁢(y)⁢ϕn+1⁢(x)-ϕn⁢(x)⁢ϕn+1⁢(y)x-y)

The reason this formula is interesting is that the left-hand side is the integral kernel for the projection operator to the subspace spanned by the polynomials {ϕi}i=0n.
